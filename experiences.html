Personal experiences

GP KIA-BARRISOL
I was a line judge of tennis during the tournament KIA-BARRISOL in Illberg in 2019. This tournament regrouped every years many players ranked in the top 100-200 ATP, these players come from the international.
This experience allowed me to acquire many skills such as: managing stress in complicated situations, respecting strict instructions, being impartial, communicating with my arbitration team.

Assistant for my tennis teacher
I was an assistant to my tennis teacher for children aged 5-6. Every Saturday from September 2019 to July 2020, it was a superb experience with good meetings and atmosphere.
Knowing that I have been practicing this sport for many years, I liked to pass on my knowledge.
Basically I wanted to do a job in sport so it was beneficial both for my training in sport option in high school but also interesting with respect to the knowledge I acquired. Such as preparing for a session or listening to the children I was teaching.
